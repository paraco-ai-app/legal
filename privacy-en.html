Privacy Policy

AI-Powered Personal Finance Coach

Last updated: April 13, 2026

1. Overview

This Privacy Policy explains how Paraco AI ("Application"), developed and published by Kılınç Labs, collects, processes, stores, and protects your personal data.

By continuing to use the Application, you confirm that you have read and accepted this policy. If you do not accept this policy, please discontinue use of the Application.

Paraco AI is an AI-powered personal finance coaching application designed for users aged 18 and above. The Application offers natural language expense logging, automatic AI categorization, and personalized financial coaching.

2. Data Controller

  • Data Controller: Kılınç Labs
  • Application: Paraco AI
  • Contact: paraco.ai@proton.me

3. Personal Data Collected

3.1 Data Collected During Registration

  • Email address (via email/password, Sign in with Apple, or Sign in with Google)
  • Name or nickname (entered during onboarding)

3.2 Automatically Collected Data

  • Push notification token (only if notification permission is granted)
  • Activity dates (for streak tracking; location data is never collected)
  • Supabase Auth JWT token for session management

3.3 User-Entered Financial Data

  • Monthly income (exact amount or range)
  • Fixed expenses (name and amount)
  • Expense records (raw text, amount, category, merchant, date)
  • Budget limits (per category)
  • Saving goals (name, target amount, current amount, monthly contribution)
  • Financial status and goal preferences (onboarding)

3.4 AI Coach Interaction Data

  • Messages sent to coaches and AI responses (chat history)
  • Text content and audio files of weekly/daily summaries
  • Monthly roast report text and audio files

4. Purposes of Processing

Collected data is processed solely for the following purposes:

  • Service delivery: Operating expense tracking, budget management, and saving goal features
  • Personalization: Enabling the AI coach to generate personalized financial advice
  • Analytics: Computing spending trends, category breakdowns, and insight reports
  • Notifications: Budget alerts, weekly summaries, and streak reminders
  • Security: Account verification and preventing unauthorized access

Your data is never used for advertising and is not shared with third-party ad networks.

5. Legal Basis for Processing

  • Performance of contract: Data processed for registration, authentication, and service delivery.
  • Legitimate interests: Data processed for service quality improvement, security infrastructure, and activity tracking.
  • Explicit consent: Permissions required for push notifications and optional voice input features. These permissions can be withdrawn at any time from the app settings.

6. Third-Party Service Providers

The following third-party providers are used to deliver the Application's services. These providers may access data only to the extent necessary for service delivery and are subject to their own privacy policies.

All third-party providers listed below — particularly AI service providers (Anthropic, OpenAI) — are contractually obligated to provide protection of user data equal to or greater than that set forth in this Privacy Policy. Kılınç Labs engages only with providers that meet these security standards.

6.1 Supabase

Infrastructure and data storage provider. All user data is stored within Supabase infrastructure.

  • Server location: United States
  • Privacy policy: supabase.com/privacy

6.2 Anthropic (Claude AI)

AI service provider used to generate personalized financial advice. The following data is transmitted to Anthropic's Claude AI service:

  • Your expense text entries (for automatic categorization)
  • Messages sent to the coach (for personalized replies)
  • Monthly income, fixed expenses, and budget limits
  • Goal and spending history summary
  • Your name (so the coach can address you)

Your email address, payment information, and account credentials are never transmitted to Anthropic. Transmitted data is used solely to process your request; Anthropic's own privacy policy and commercial terms govern how this data is retained and processed.

  • Server location: United States
  • Privacy policy: anthropic.com/privacy

6.3 OpenAI

Voice synthesis service provider. Only AI-generated text content is transmitted; raw user data is not sent.

  • Server location: United States
  • Privacy policy: openai.com/policies/privacy-policy

6.4 RevenueCat

Subscription and purchase management provider. User ID and App Store / Google Play purchase information are processed.

  • Server location: United States
  • Privacy policy: revenuecat.com/privacy

6.5 Apple and Google (Authentication)

Optional OAuth sign-in providers. Each provider's own privacy standards apply.

  • Apple: apple.com/legal/privacy
  • Google: policies.google.com/privacy

6.6 Expo / EAS

Application distribution infrastructure provider.

  • Privacy policy: expo.dev/privacy

7. International Data Transfers

Supabase, Anthropic, OpenAI, and RevenueCat servers are located in the United States. Your data is therefore transferred internationally. Necessary technical and administrative measures are taken for such transfers.

8. Data Security

  • Authentication tokens (JWT) are stored encrypted on your device using expo-secure-store.
  • The Application does not use web cookies.
  • Premium membership fields can only be modified server-side; client-side direct access is disabled.
  • Supabase Row Level Security (RLS) ensures each user can only access their own data.

9. Data Retention and Deletion

Your data is retained for as long as your account is active. Account deletion is permanent and cannot be reversed.

When "Delete Account" is activated, the following data is permanently deleted:

  • Profile information
  • Expense records
  • Fixed expenses
  • Budget limits
  • Saving goals
  • Chat messages
  • Audio summaries and roast reports
  • Weekly challenge records
  • Activity streak records
  • All audio files (Supabase Storage)

9.1 Revoking AI Consent

On first launch of the Application, you are asked to provide explicit consent for sharing your data with Anthropic's Claude AI service. To revoke this consent, you can use either of the following:

  • Delete your account: Permanently deleting your account via Settings → Delete Account removes all your data and stops AI data sharing.
  • Email request: Write to paraco.ai@proton.me to request that AI features be disabled for your account. Requests are processed within 30 days. In that case AI-dependent features of the Application (automatic categorization, coach chat, weekly summary, insights) will no longer be available.

Once consent is revoked, data previously transmitted to Anthropic is deleted per Anthropic's own retention policy.

10. Notifications and Permissions

10.1 Push Notifications

If notification permission is granted, the following four independent notification types become active; each can be toggled on or off individually:

  • Weekly summary notification
  • Daily reminder
  • Budget alert
  • Streak reminder

Unless permission is granted, no push token is stored and no notifications are sent.

10.2 Microphone and Speech Recognition

Microphone and speech recognition permissions are requested separately for voice expense input. Even if denied, all text-based features remain fully available.

11. Users Under 18

Paraco AI is intended for users aged 18 and above. We do not knowingly collect personal data from individuals under 18. If you believe a minor has registered, please contact paraco.ai@proton.me and the account will be deleted immediately.

12. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Learn whether your personal data is being processed
  • Request information about how it is processed
  • Learn the purpose of processing and whether it is used accordingly
  • Know which third parties your data has been transferred to
  • Request correction of incomplete or inaccurate data
  • Request deletion or destruction of your data
  • Object to processing
  • Request compensation for damages arising from unlawful processing

Submit requests to paraco.ai@proton.me. Requests will be resolved within 30 days of submission.

13. Account Security

You are responsible for providing accurate and up-to-date registration information, and for maintaining the confidentiality of your account credentials. Please notify paraco.ai@proton.me immediately if you detect unauthorized access to your account.

14. Data Accuracy

The accuracy and currency of financial data entered into the Application is your responsibility. Kılınç Labs is not liable for the outcomes of financial decisions based on AI advice generated from inaccurate or incomplete data.

15. Changes to This Policy

Kılınç Labs reserves the right to update this Privacy Policy without prior notice. Material changes will be communicated via in-app notification or registered email. Continued use of the Application after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
